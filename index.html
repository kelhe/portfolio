Story so far...

Before transitioning into software development, I worked in front-facing jobs such as an Emergency Medical Technician (EMT) and manager in the nightlife industry. These jobs were rewarding in their own way, as they allowed me to help others and connect with people from all walks of life. However, I began to feel like I was hitting the ceiling in terms of career growth and personal development.

I realized that I was not actively learning new skills, and that mental stagnation helped me determine that I needed to change careers to continue growing as a professional. Luckily, I had a childhood passion for technology and programming, and I decided to pursue this interest by joining an online bootcamp to start learning. Since then, I have embarked on a continuous learning process, that never fails to bring me satisfaction when my ideas come to life through code.

The move has allowed me to combine my passion for technology with my desire for personal and professional growth. It has been a challenging and rewarding experience, and I look forward to the opportunities that lie ahead.
